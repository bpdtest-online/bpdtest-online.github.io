Understanding the BPD Test

The BPD test is a screening tool designed to assess symptoms associated with Borderline Personality Disorder. This online assessment helps individuals identify potential signs of BPD and determine whether professional evaluation might be beneficial.

Important Notice: This BPD test is for screening purposes only and is not a diagnostic tool. Only a licensed mental health professional can provide an official BPD diagnosis through clinical evaluation.

Scientific Basis

Our BPD test is based on the McLean Screening Instrument for BPD (MSI-BPD) and diagnostic criteria outlined in the DSM-5.

Comprehensive Assessment

The test evaluates key criteria of BPD, including emotional instability, relationship patterns, self-image issues, and impulsivity.

Immediate Results

After completing the BPD test, you'll receive instant feedback about your symptom patterns and guidance on next steps.

What Does the BPD Test Measure?

The test assesses various aspects of personality and behavior patterns commonly associated with BPD:

  • Emotional instability and mood swings
  • Fear of abandonment
  • Self-image and identity issues
  • Impulsive behaviors
  • Thoughts of self-harm
  • Chronic feelings of emptiness
  • Anger management
  • Stress-related symptoms
